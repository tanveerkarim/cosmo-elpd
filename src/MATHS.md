# Formulae used in this subfolder 

## Pivot Redshift, $z_p$
- Pivot redshift, $z_p$ or zp, refers to the redshift at which the covariance between the dark energy paramters, w0 and wa, is minimized, i.e, Cov(w0, wa) = 0
- CPL parameterization: $w(z) = w_0 + wa (1-a)$ 
- Pivot parametrization: $w(z) = w_p + wa (a_p - a)$ 
- Relationship between w0 and wp: $w_p = w_0 + w_a (1 - a_p)$
- Pivot scale factor: $a_p = 1 + \frac{cov(w_0, w_a)}{var(w_a)}$
